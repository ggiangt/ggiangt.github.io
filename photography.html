Photography

I pursue photography as a hobby to capture the beauty of landscapes, cityscapes, and everyday scenes, often using my Nikon D5300 or iPhone. Photography sharpens my eye for composition, color, and the subtle details of life, which also enhances my painting skills. I've had the privilege of photographing for events of various scales, blending art and storytelling through the lens.
