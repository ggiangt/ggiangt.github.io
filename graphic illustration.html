Design & Illustration

My design journey started around Middle School, when I attempted to download a pirated version of Photoshop on my first ever laptop. From spending days and nights figuring out these complicated interfaces of design softwares, my curiosity and perseverance have allowed me to lead the production of the schools' yearbook teams, initiate marketing campaigns for nonprofit organizations, design brand identity for local businesses, etc.

Featured Works

Unity In Motion

Digital illustration with Procreate, printed on a 17ft x 5ft banner in Ron Edyt Village

I was commissioned by the University of Waterloo to create this community mural, where I potrayed the vibrant, bustling Waterloo campus divided into three regions—the Ion LRT, school buildings, and a cosmic expanse. At its heart, the motto "Together we create" highlights Waterloo's innovative spirit and the power of simple, authentic connections. Diverse students, iconic buildings like the Dana Porter Library and Engineering 7, and a faculty-inspired color palette infuse the artwork with vibrancy and life to celebrate the values of equity, diversity and inclusion.

Beyond Infinities

Desinged with Photoshop & Procreate


*Winner of the 2024 GBDA Design Competition

Set in a space-like, gravity-free backdrop, the design symbolizes the boundless opportunities offered by the GBDA program, where creativity, technology, and business converge. Dynamic characters represent its students, with vivid streams of color illustrating the diverse skillsets they develop. Reflecting on my own journey, this piece showcases how I aim to bring cultures, emotions, and experiences to reality with my creations.

St.Michaels  University School’s 2022-23 Yearbook Cover Design

Created with Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator

In Grade 11 and 12, I proudly held the role as the Chief Editor of my high school’s Yearbook Team, where I spearheaded the production of the annual 160-page publication, from ideation to output with a team of 20 members. Creating an exceptional yearbook goes beyond design—it demands critical thinking, data-driven decisions, and empathy. More importantly, I discovered my passion for crafting memorable experiences and inspiring others to follow their creative pursuits. With this cover, I was also the Recipient of the British Columbia Provincial Yearbook Cover Design Award.
